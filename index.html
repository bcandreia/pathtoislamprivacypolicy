Privacy Policy for Path to Islam

Last updated: May 15, 2025

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Location is used only to calculate prayer times and find nearby mosques. This data is used solely on the device and is never transmitted or saved.

The app may send optional prayer time notifications. It does not include any ads, tracking, analytics, or third-party content. No account creation or personal information is required to use this app.
